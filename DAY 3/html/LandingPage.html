Welcome to Big Buck Bunny

A light-hearted animated adventure about a gentle giant who finally stands up for himself.

ABOUT THE MOVIE

Big Buck Bunny is a comedy-driven animated short featuring a peaceful and kind-hearted rabbit who lives in a colorful forest. His quiet life is turned upside down when a mischievous trio of rodents start causing chaos — and Big Buck Bunny decides it’s finally time to take a stand. The film is known for its charming humor, beautiful animation, and expressive characters.

Key Highlights

  • Genre: Animation / Comedy / Short
  • Story Theme: Kindness, courage, and standing up against bullies
  • Runtime: ~10 minutes
  • Production: Created with open-source animation technology
  • Audience: Family-friendly

CHARACTERS

Big Buck Bunny

A gentle giant of the forest. He loves sunbathing, gardening, and butterflies. Despite his size, he’s soft-hearted — until pushed too far.

Frank (the leader of the bullies)

Sneaky, loud, and always looking for trouble. Frank leads the trio of forest pranksters.

Rinky

A small rodent with big energy. Loves chaotic fun and causing mayhem.

Gamera

Sly and slow-moving but full of bad ideas. Often the brains behind the trio’s pranks.

STORY

The film follows a peaceful bunny enjoying his day in a vibrant forest. Three troublemaking rodents interrupt his calm life with cruel pranks. After tolerating their mischief for too long, the bunny finally decides to fight back — but in his own comedic and clever way. The story unfolds with humor, fast-paced action, and a satisfying moment of justice.
